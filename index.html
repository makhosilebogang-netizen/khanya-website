At Khanya Reading & Writing Lessons, we believe that a child’s ability to read with understanding and express themselves through writing is the foundation of academic success.

Across South Africa, many learners struggle in school not because they lack intelligence or potential, but because they have not mastered the essential skills of reading for meaning and writing clearly. As a result, they fall behind in theory-based subjects such as History, Natural Sciences, and English — subjects that require understanding, interpretation, and written expression.

This is not just a challenge; it is a growing crisis. Khanya Reading & Writing Lessons was established in response to this need — to offer parents a practical, focused solution and to support learners in building the skills they need to succeed. Our aim is not only to improve reading and writing, but to help learners gain confidence, perform better academically, and develop a genuine love for learning.

ABOUT US!

Our approach is rooted in both training and passion. I completed literacy studies in my first year at North-West University and earned a certificate from PeneBotshelo Writers Foundation, where I was trained in the art of teaching reading with understanding and writing with confidence.

Based in Mahikeng in the North West Province, Khanya Reading & Writing Lessons is committed to equipping learners with strong foundational skills that will support their academic journey and open doors for their future.

This is more than tutoring. It is a commitment to helping learners succeed, grow in confidence, and discover the power of reading and writing.

OUR SERVICES

Reading & Comprehension Support

We help learners improve their reading skills step by step, from basic word recognition to fluent reading. Learners are taught how to understand what they read, answer questions, and explain ideas clearly.

Writing Skills Development

We teach learners how to write correctly and confidently. This includes building sentences, improving grammar, and developing paragraphs and short pieces of writing.

Personalised Lessons (Online & Home)

We offer flexible lessons tailored to each learner’s level. Sessions are available online or through house visits, with focused support to help each learner improve at their own pace.
